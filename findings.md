## Phase 1 — Blueprint Research

### Design Direction
- **Style:** Clean, modern, Linear/Notion-inspired — premium SaaS feel
- **Palette:** Dark slate + amber/gold accents — signals luxury and automotive excellence
- **Approach:** Lots of whitespace, soft shadows, rounded corners, subtle micro-animations

### Tech Stack Decision
- **React + Vite:** Fast dev server, modern tooling, production builds
- **Tailwind CSS:** Utility-first, rapid prototyping, easy responsive design
- **No backend:** Static site, contact form is front-end only (no API)

### Car Detailing Industry Observations
- Premium feel is critical — customers associate website quality with service quality
- Hero section must show transformation/results (before/after or glossy car imagery)
- Trust signals (logos, testimonials) are high-converting for local service businesses
- Contact form is the primary conversion point
